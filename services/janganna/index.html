Janganna (डिजिटल जनगणना) Kya Hai?

Haryana me digital जनगणना ke liye self-enumeration facility shuru ho chuki hai. Ab eligible households official portal par jaakar apni family aur housing details online bhar sakte hain, phir unhe ek Self-Enumeration ID (SE ID) milti hai jo field visit ke time enumerator ko dikhani hoti hai. Yeh process Census of India 2027 ke Phase I yani Houselisting and Housing Census ka part hai.

Official portal se.census.gov.in par state select karke mobile OTP ke through login hota hai. PIB aur official portal ke hisaab se Haryana ke liye self-enumeration window 16 April 2026 se 30 April 2026 tak hai, aur iske baad 1 May se 30 May 2026 tak enumerator house-to-house verification ke liye aayega. Portal par 33 notified questions, housing amenities, household details aur assets ki information fill ki jati hai. Census Act, 1948 ke under data confidential rakha jata hai.

Iske Fayde

  • Ghar baithe official portal par family aur house listing details bhar sakte hain
  • SE ID generate hoti hai jisse field verification faster ho jata hai
  • Digital process se data entry zyada accurate aur transparent hoti hai
  • Future planning me school, road, water, welfare aur local infrastructure decisions ko support milta hai

Kaise Karein – Step by Step

  1. 1Official portal https://se.census.gov.in par jaayein
  2. 2State/UT me Haryana select karein aur mobile number se OTP verify karein
  3. 3Household aur housing related questions dhyan se fill karein
  4. 4Form submit karke generated Self-Enumeration ID (SE ID) note ya save karein
  5. 5Enumerator jab ghar aaye to verification ke liye SE ID share karein

Zaruri Tips

  • Ek household ke liye ek hi mobile number use karein, official portal 6 AM se 12 Midnight tak available hai
  • Self-enumeration optional hai, online form bharne ke baad bhi enumerator verification ke liye aayega
  • Bank OTP, bank account number ya zabardasti documents maangne wale fraud attempts se bach kar rahen
  • Income, Aadhaar ya mobile details dena har case me mandatory nahi hota; sirf verified census process par hi bharosa karein
  • SE ID ko screenshot, print ya WhatsApp par save kar lena practical rahega

Aksar Puchhe Jaane Wale Sawal

जनगणना self-enumeration Haryana ke liye official portal kya hai?

Official portal se.census.gov.in hai jahan Census of India 2027 ki self-enumeration facility available hai.

Kya online form bharne ke baad bhi koi ghar par aayega?

Haan. Official process ke mutabik self-enumeration ke baad bhi enumerator field verification ke liye ghar aata hai aur aapka SE ID maang sakta hai.

Haryana me self-enumeration ki dates kya hain?

Official information ke hisaab se Haryana me self-enumeration 16 April 2026 se 30 April 2026 tak available hai. Iske baad 1 May se 30 May 2026 tak house-to-house operations hote hain.

Kya is process me bank balance, OTP ya private financial details deni hoti hain?

Nahi. Census process me bank OTP ya bank account details maangne wale attempts suspicious ho sakte hain. Sirf official portal aur verified enumerator process par hi trust karein.

Nain Photostate isme kya help kar sakta hai?

Hum portal access, mobile OTP login, question understanding, form filling guidance aur SE ID save/print me practical help de sakte hain.

Hum Aapki Madad Kar Sakte Hain
