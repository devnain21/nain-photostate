Voter ID Card Kya Hai?

Voter ID Card (EPIC) voting ke liye zaruri hai aur important identity proof bhi hai. National Voters Service Portal (NVSP) se aap new voter card ke liye apply kar sakte hain, correction karva sakte hain ya e-EPIC download kar sakte hain.

Voter ID Card (EPIC) se aap apni vote ka istemal kar sakte hain aur yeh India ka ek major identity proof bhi hai. Ab digital e-EPIC bhi available hai.

Iske Fayde

  • Democracy me participate karne ka haq
  • Strong government ID proof hai
  • e-EPIC phone me rakh sakte hain
  • Address aur age proof ke roop me bhi

Kaise Karein – Step by Step

  1. 1Voters portal par login karein
  2. 2New Registration (Form 6) ya Correction select karein
  3. 3Personal details bharein
  4. 4Photo aur documents upload karein
  5. 5Submit karein
  6. 6BLO verification ke baad card banega

Zaruri Tips

  • 18 saal hote hi turant apply karein
  • Address change hone par update zarur karvaayein
  • Election se pehle voter list me apna naam check karein

Aksar Puchhe Jaane Wale Sawal

Voter ID banane ke liye kitni umar chahiye?

1 January ko 18 saal complete hone chahiye us saal ke liye.

e-EPIC kya hai?

e-EPIC digital voter ID card hai jo aap apne phone me rakh sakte hain. PDF format me download hota hai.

Hum Aapki Madad Kar Sakte Hain

Agar aapko Voter ID Card me koi problem aa rahi hai ya aap khud se nahi kar pa rahe, to Nain CSC Center (Nain Photostate Danoda), Jind par aayein. Hamare trained operators aapka poora kaam karwa denge - form filling se lekar final submission tak.
