CEIR Mobile Complaint Kya Hai?

CEIR (Central Equipment Identity Register) se aap apne chori ya khoye hue mobile phone ko block karva sakte hain. IMEI number se phone track bhi hota hai. Police complaint ke saath CEIR par bhi complaint daalni chahiye.

Phone chori ya kho gaya? CEIR portal se phone ka IMEI block hota hai aur woh kisi bhi network par kaam nahi karega. Chor ke liye bekar ho jaata hai phone.

Iske Fayde

  • Chori ka phone sabhi networks par block hota hai
  • Chor ke liye phone bekar ho jaata hai
  • Phone milne par wapas unblock bhi ho sakta hai
  • Police complaint ke saath dual protection

Kaise Karein – Step by Step

  1. 1CEIR portal par jaayein
  2. 2Block Lost/Stolen Phone select karein
  3. 3IMEI number daalein
  4. 4Personal details bharein
  5. 5Police complaint details daalein
  6. 6Phone sabhi networks par block ho jaayega

Zaruri Tips

  • IMEI number phone ke box par ya *#06# se milta hai
  • Police FIR pehle se darz karvaayein
  • Phone mila to unblock request bhi CEIR se karein

Aksar Puchhe Jaane Wale Sawal

IMEI number kaise pata karein?

Apne phone se *#06# dial karein ya phone ki box par likha hota hai.

Kya block kiya hua phone wapas unblock ho sakta hai?

Haan, agar phone mil jaaye to CEIR portal se unblock request de sakte hain police verification ke baad.

Hum Aapki Madad Kar Sakte Hain
